Who I Am

Profile

“An extrovert with good interpersonal skills — building stronger cultures with cross-team collaboration, inclusion and engagement.”

I’m Shubhada — a Product Strategist, communicator, and lifelong learner. I began as a Mechanical Engineer, pivoted to management consulting, landed in Munich for my Masters, and found my true calling at the intersection of product strategy and human connection.

My work is about more than deliverables. Whether it’s curating a training programme, presenting at an expo, or moderating a workshop across time zones — I believe people and communication are always at the heart of great outcomes.

Outside of work, I’m a dancer, a traveller, a reader, and an abstract photographer who sees stories in everything. 27 countries later, I’m still curious.
